Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a state in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is the sixth-most populous state in India, with a population of about 1.2 million people. The state is bounded by the Arabian Sea to the north, the Bay of Bengal to the south, and the mainland India to the east and west. Tamil Nadu is known for its beaches, forests, and wildlife. The state is also known for its architecture, which is influenced by the Tamil culture.
